about

Hello and welcome to a small section about myself. I reside in the great northwest, in the the state of Washington. When I am not working I try to travel as often as I can. My wife and I have two spoiled dogs that know how to game the system to get anything they want.

My developer career came fairly late in life, of 2012. My background is computer animation and after 12 years in the video game industry I switched my career from creating 3D art to coding UI and UX. But even working as an artist I have always had a love for some form of coding, specifically HTML and CSS. From time to time I would build website for family and friends.

But it wasn't until after I left my heavily art focused world behind and started working on Windows Phone and then Xbox, that I finally found the discipline as a UX Engineer. Focusing on design and implementing that vision in the product made sense to me. It was a way that I could champion the work of the design to bring that vision to life inside the product.

I consider myself a self taught developer and learning as I build one project at a time. I have definitely been blessed and fortunate to work and learn with awesome developers on all of my projects. One thing I learn as developer is you never stop learning.
