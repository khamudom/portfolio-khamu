Microsoft Edge

Collaborated with the design team to build web components for multiple experiences for Microsoft Edge, Edge Shopping and Microsoft News

Edge shopping

Edge has a built in feature to share multiple ways with online shoppers to help save money.

Working closely with the design team, I built a few components that were used in this feature. I contributed the coupon, cashback and shopping-recommendation components. The components are then consumed and implemented by our devloping partners.

Edge news feed

The browser has a customizable news feed feature that allows users to see news articles and other content from their favorite websites and news sources.

I helped iterate on the article component. In addition, I updated the native-ad and info-pane-ad components, that are used specifically for ads within the feed.

Vertical tabs

One big feature on Edge is the ability to set vertical tabs. It allows the users to view tabs on the side of the browser instead of up top.

Working closely with the project manager and the motion designer, I created a prototype to help the team find the best animation when the tab panel slides out in the unpinned state.
