Xbox One

Collaborated with the Xbox Console Design team to help them understand the product capabilities and limits. In addition work along side the development team to create and implement templates. Worked on multiple iterations of the UI from the launch of Xbox One to the console release of the Xbox One S and X.

Dashboard and Details Pages

Working with the Interaction, Motion and Visual design team to help implement their design vision into the Xbox One.

At launch my main focus was building the games and app details and subscription pages. Also assisted in the Home and Store dashboard UI.

Launched Dashboard - 2013

Iterations

Further iteration I took on owning the Dashboard, specifically the Store twist, and in addition, the Gold Lounge and My Games and Apps pages.

Architecture

The UI is part of Xbox Shell and the language used to build our UI Views is XAML. We also used the MVVM architecture.

Technologies

  • XAML
  • XAML data binding
  • MVVM consumption
  • Compass
